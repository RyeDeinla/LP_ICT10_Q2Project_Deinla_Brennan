Welcome!

This is the BSCD or the Bicol School for Children with Disabilities. It is our goal to provide quality education to persons with disabilities in the Bicol region (PWDs), ensuring that they learn in a conducive, efficient, and fun environment that helps them propagate their skills despite their struggles. With our meticulously crafted and curated classrooms and teaching areas along with our well-trained teachers, you have an educator's promise that here, no matter what you are experiencing, you will be loved and taught with passion and care.
